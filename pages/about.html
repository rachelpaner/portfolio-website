A little about me.

I was born and raised in the Bay Area and am currently studying Cognitive Science with a Specialization in Design & Interaction at UC San Diego.

With a long history of involvement in visual arts and music, I've expressed myself through drawing, painting, choir, digital art, violin, and many other things that bring me creative joy.

This love for imaginative displays translates into bringing that same joy to others by addressing their needs with a tailored interface that combines both form and function.

And a little about my pursuits, projects, and pastimes!

UCSD Sigma Eta Pi

I'm the VP of Design for UCSD's premier co-ed entrepreneurship fraternity, designing a website, branding guidelines, and graphics that aim to inspire education, innovation, and collaboration.

UCSD CSE Society

I'm the Design Chair for UCSD's oldest computing student organization, managing internal and external design deliverables across 4 sub-orgs in different aspects of tech career development.

UCSD Campus Events

I'm a Lead Marketing/Graphic Design Asst. at UCSD's primary performing arts and events department, leading my team of designers to promote art that attracts, engages, and delights audiences across San Diego.

front-end development

I enjoy inspecting the details of the development side. I'm on the road towards becoming a full-stack designer, whether it be coding my own portfolio or learning REACT through 100Devs.

project management

Through my leadership positions and as a UX Design Intern at SAP, I'm on a journey that goes beyond charts and timelines, orchestrating creativity, structure, and teamwork to craft efficiency from chaos.

animation

I always look for opportunities to infuse my designs with motion, from micro-interactions to animated shorts. I can spend hours in After Effects, adjusting every keyframe and pixel to bring graphics to life.

crochet

The best way to spend a rainy afternoon is crocheting a seemingly never-ending sweater while watching a cheesy '90s rom-com :)

jewelry-making

I've been making beaded jewelery for over 5 years, and run a small shop on Instagram! This passion project was my first foray into branding and graphic design, and holds a special place in my heart.

lifting

If I'm not at home or school, you can usually find me at the gym! Lifting keeps me motivated, and I love the feeling of getting stronger both physically and mentally.
